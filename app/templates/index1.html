6007 began in 2013, at Millikan Science Academy. We have been competing since Sack Attack and have competed at worlds four times, during Toss Up, Skyrise, Starstruck, and In the Zone. We are best known for winning the Science Division in 2017, and for winning the California State Championship twice. 6007 is also devoted to giving back to the community, providing mentorship to younger teams, and remaining available to answer teams’ questions.

Seasons

In 2018, 6007X was able to place 17 in the world in the Robot Skills Ranking, as well as coming in second in the Promote Award Challenge.

In 2017, by far the most sucessful year of 6007, in the game Vex Starstruck, was able to rank 5th in the world in High School.

In 6007's first year of high school competition, Nothing But Net, the team struggled with a cortex failure at states and was unable to qualify for worlds.

In 2015, Skyrise,the team made it to worlds and went undefeated for the first 5 matches, the only middle team to do so. However, an untimely fall rendered the robot almost useless and the team lost three of their remaining matches, barely missing the cutoff for elimination.

In 2014, 6007's first year of serious competition, Toss Up, the team made it to worlds quarterfinals but was eliminated by the eventual world champions.
